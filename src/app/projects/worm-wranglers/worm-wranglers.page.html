Projects Worm Wranglers An Asymmetrical Racing Game Where One Player Controls The Track
What is Worm Wranglers? Worm Wranglers is a two-to-four-player racing game that takes place in a colorful, low-poly garden. One to three players control beetle karts and attempt to stay on a moving earthworm “racetrack” as long as they can, while another player controls the worm, twisting and turning to try and shake them off. The game ends either when all the beetles have fallen off the worm, or the worm crashes into an obstacle in the garden. My primary roles were 3D modeling for the worm (head / tail) and programming for worm generation, beetle customization, and game flow.
